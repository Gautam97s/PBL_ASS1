About Steam

Steam is a digital distribution platform developed by Valve Corporation, primarily for video games. Launched in 2003, it has become one of the largest and most influential platforms for gaming and software distribution worldwide. Initially created to offer automatic updates for Valve's own games, Steam quickly expanded to include third-party titles, becoming a central hub for PC gaming.The platform allows users to purchase, download, and play a wide variety of games across different genres, from indie games to major AAA titles. Steam offers a digital storefront where users can browse, review, and buy games, often featuring discounted sales during special events, making it highly popular among gamers. Additionally, the platform supports community engagement through forums, reviews, and the Steam Workshop, where players can share user-generated content such as mods and game assets.
