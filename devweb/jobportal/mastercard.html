About MasterCard

Mastercard is a global financial services corporation founded in 1966, headquartered in Purchase, New York. Renowned for its innovative payment solutions, Mastercard operates one of the world's largest electronic payment networks, facilitating transactions in more than 210 countries. The company's core business revolves around processing card payments between banks and merchants, ensuring secure and efficient payment transactions.Mastercard provides a range of products and services, including credit, debit, and prepaid cards, as well as digital wallets and contactless payment solutions. The company is committed to leveraging technology to enhance customer experiences, offering solutions like Mastercard Identity Check for secure online transactions and Mastercard Send for fast person-to-person payments.
